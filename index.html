My Top 3 Memories of Baltimore ( 3rd Grade Edition )

  1. Memory #1

    When I first began my year living with my grandma in Baltimore for 3rd Grade, she signed me up for a community center summer camp-go home program.

    We took a few field trips every now and again but the most memorable for me was the Orioles baseball game, I don't really remember who they were up against really but it was my first sports game that I watched live and got to be around some friends and have fun until the sun set...A great day I say.

  2. Memory #2

    This was a cool and memorable Halloween for me, asked for a costume based on Yokai Watch but ended up going outside as Black Goku instead but it was still really cool.

    That is until I headed home with my haul and was dying from tick damage since the Goku wig was itchy...figures.

  3. Memory #3

    I was invited to my first sleepover by a friend for their birthday back then. It was an absolutely great experience for me and I had so much fun. ( I was also the first to fall asleep but don't worry, I was safe other than having to chug half a gallon of milk, it was easy after all. )
